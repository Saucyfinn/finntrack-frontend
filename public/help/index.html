How to Track Your Boat

Get your phone sending GPS positions so spectators can follow you live

Option 1: Browser Tracking Easiest

Use our Join Race page directly in your phone's browser. No app to install!

  1. Open finntracker.org/join on your phone
  2. Select your boat from the fleet list (or enter manually)
  3. Select the race(s) you're participating in
  4. Tap Start Tracking and allow GPS permission
  5. Keep the page open while sailing - it updates every 2 seconds
Tip: Keep your phone plugged in and screen on. Disable auto-lock in Settings to prevent the page from stopping.

Option 2: Traccar Client App Recommended

Free GPS tracking app that runs reliably in the background. Best battery life and most accurate.

  1. Download Traccar Client from App Store or Google Play
  2. Open the app and configure these settings:
Device identifier: YOUR_SAIL_NUMBER (e.g., AUS123)
Server URL: https://api.finntracker.org/traccar?key=finn123
Frequency: 2 (seconds)
Distance: 0
Angle: 0
  1. Toggle the Service status switch to ON
  2. Allow location permission (Always or While Using)
  3. Your position will now appear on the Live Tracking page!

Option 3: OwnTracks App

Another reliable GPS tracking app with background tracking support.

  1. Download OwnTracks from App Store or Google Play
  2. Go to Settings and configure:
Mode: HTTP
URL: https://api.finntracker.org/owntracks
DeviceID: YOUR_SAIL_NUMBER

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know my tracking is working?

Open the Live page on another device and look for your boat marker on the map. Your sail number should appear in the boat list with a green "active" status.

Can I track on multiple races at once?

Yes! On the Join Race page, you can select multiple races (hold Ctrl/Cmd and click). Your position will be sent to all selected races simultaneously.

What about battery life?

The Traccar app is most battery-efficient. For browser tracking, keep your phone plugged in. A typical race day uses about 30-50% battery with GPS tracking.

Does it work without cell signal?

GPS works without cell signal, but you need data connection to send positions. If you lose signal, positions are queued and sent when connection returns (Traccar app only).

Can spectators on shore watch me?

Yes! Share the link finntracker.org with anyone. They can select the race and see all boats tracking in real-time on the map.

What's my Device ID / Sail Number?

Use your sail number without spaces (e.g., AUS123, NZL54, GBR4). This is how your boat appears on the tracker.
